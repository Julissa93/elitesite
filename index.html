Our Mission

We are ELiTe, which stands for Empowering Ladies In Technology, and we are a student run organization at CUNY's College of Staten Island. Our mission is to provide a supportive environment for women who want to pursue degrees in STEM as well as careers in the tech industry. We want to tackle the gender gap head on and encourage more women to pursue degrees and careers in the tech industry.
